Since April 2023, I have been serving as an **Assistant Manager** at the **Autonomous AI and Decision Support Laboratory**, part of the **Artificial Intelligence Technology Centre** (AITeC) at the **National Centre for Physics** (NCP), Pakistan. I hold an MPhil in **Microelectronic Engineering and Semiconductor Physics** (2018) from the University of the Punjab, Lahore, where I achieved a thesis score of 96%, and a BSc in **Electronic Engineering** (2014) from The Islamia University of Bahawalpur, where I secured 98% marks in **FYP**.

In 2021, I independently pursued and mastered **Deep Reinforcement Learning** (DRL), subsequently offering freelance research and solution development services on Upwork and Fiverr. In January 2023, I was selected for a prestigious **AI Fellowship** at AITeC, which involved intensive training and evaluation in DRL, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), computer vision, Edge AI, and deployment tools such as Docker and Kubernetes. The fellowship also included advanced coursework in mathematics and statistics. Upon completion, I was awarded a three-year Assistant Manager contract, recognizing my performance and potential in the field of AI research and development.

I am currently open to **PhD collaborations and research opportunities** starting in Spring 2026. If you're working on robotics, learning based control systems or multi-agent autonomous systems, I'd be excited to connect.
